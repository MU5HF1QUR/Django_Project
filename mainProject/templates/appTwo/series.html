Some Great Action Series

One Punch Man

Saitama, a superhero who can defeat any enemy with a single punch, seeks excitement and purpose in a world filled with powerful adversaries.

Naruto

A young ninja named Naruto aims to become the strongest ninja in his village, facing powerful enemies and uncovering his true destiny.

Bleach

Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager with the ability to see ghosts, becomes a Soul Reaper tasked with protecting humanity from evil spirits.

Dragon Ball Z

Join Goku and his allies as they defend Earth from powerful extraterrestrial threats, engaging in intense martial arts battles.

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

Two brothers embark on a quest to find the Philosopher's Stone, encountering alchemy, conspiracy, and dark forces.

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

A young demon slayer named Tanjiro fights against demons while searching for a cure to turn his sister back into a human.

Fate/stay night: Unlimited Blade Works

In a battle royale, mages summon heroic spirits to fight for the ultimate prize: the Holy Grail.

My Hero Academia

In a world where almost everyone has superpowers, a young boy trains to become a hero and protect the innocent.

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

Generations of the Joestar family use their unique abilities to battle supernatural threats across different time periods.

Some Great Sci-fi Series

Steins;Gate

A group of eccentric scientists discovers the ability to send messages to the past, leading to a thrilling race against time to prevent impending disasters.

Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex

Set in a future where cybernetic enhancements are the norm, a team of elite agents investigates crimes involving advanced technology.

Neon Genesis Evangelion

In a post-apocalyptic world, teenagers pilot giant robots to defend humanity against mysterious and powerful beings known as Angels.

Psycho-Pass

In a dystopian society, a group of law enforcement officers uses a powerful system to detect individuals with criminal intentions.

Cowboy Bebop

Follow the misadventures of a bounty hunter and his eclectic crew aboard their spaceship, chasing elusive targets across the galaxy.

Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion

Lelouch, a young genius, obtains the power to control minds and orchestrates a rebellion against a powerful empire.

Serial Experiments Lain

Lain, a socially isolated girl, becomes entangled in a series of mysteries and conspiracies involving the Wired, a virtual realm.

Attack on Titan

Humanity battles giant humanoid creatures known as Titans, as a group of young warriors fights to reclaim their world.

Gurren Lagann

In an underground village, two friends unearth a giant robot and embark on a journey to the surface, facing formidable enemies.

Some Great Adventures Series

One Piece

Follow the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew as they search for the ultimate treasure, the One Piece.

Hunter x Hunter

A young boy named Gon aspires to become a Hunter, a prestigious title that brings adventure, danger, and discovery.

Avatar: The Last Airbender

In a world divided into elemental nations, a young boy with the power to control all elements embarks on a journey to save his world.

Fairy Tail

Join the members of the Fairy Tail Guild, a group of mages with diverse abilities, as they embark on exciting quests and face powerful adversaries.

The Promised Neverland

A group of orphans discovers the horrifying truth behind their peaceful orphanage and plans a daring escape.

Mushishi

Ginko, a wandering traveler, encounters mystical creatures known as Mushi and explores their influence on the human world.

The Rising of the Shield Hero

Summoned to a parallel world, a hero with the power of a legendary shield must navigate political intrigue and battle to save the realm.

Magi: The Labyrinth of Magic

Follow Aladdin and his friends as they explore dungeons, obtain magical artifacts, and unravel the secrets of the world.

Made in Abyss

A young girl and her robot friend venture into a treacherous, mysterious abyss in search of her missing mother.
