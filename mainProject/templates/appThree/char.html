Monkey D. Luffy (One Piece)

Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates in the One Piece series. He possesses a rubber-like body after eating a Devil Fruit, granting him incredible elasticity and strength. Luffy is characterized by his trademark straw hat, carefree personality, and his quest to find the legendary treasure, One Piece.
